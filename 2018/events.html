Attendance to the guided tour

Unfortunately, we can only offer a limited amount of places for the tour. This means that the free places will be covered by order of registration, except for applicants that will have preference. The attending and waiting list for the guided tour of the Botanical Garden will be sent the the weekend before the conference. In order to guarantee that people in the attending list attend, there will be a symbolic fee of two euros for those attending that has to be paid at the registration desk.

Wine and Cheese

The Wine and Cheese is an informal event where applicants, Berlin students and other conference participants can socialize ina relaxed environment. As its name indicates, this social event is accompanied by cheese and wine that has been selected by the organizers for the event. Nevertheless, there will be non-alcoholic options for those conference participants that don't drink alcohol.

The Wine and Cheese will take place at the BMS Lounge in the Technische Universitát Berlin. More information about its location can be found in this link.

Every participant is invited to join the Wine and Cheese.
