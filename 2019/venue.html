Venue

Lecture hall MA 141 (talks)

The talks of 7th BMS Student Conference will take place at the Institut für Mathematik (Faculty of Mathematics) of the Technische Universität Berlin, which is located at Str. des 17. Juni 136, 10623 Berlin. All talks will take place in lecture hall MA 141: it is located on the first floor (close to the first elevators you reach from the first entrance, turn left at the elevators and you will see the catering for our conference).

BMS Lounge (Wine and Cheese)

The BMS Lounge is where the Wine and Cheese event will take place. It is located at Institut für Mathematik (Faculty of Mathematics) of the Technische Universität Berlin, where it can be found at room MA209 in the second floor. It is located at Str. des 17. Juni 136, 10623 Berlin.

Where to eat?

There are several fast and cheap option around the campus of TU Berlin. These are some of them:

  • TU Mensa Hardenbergstrasse (2 euros; payment only by Mensa card)
  • TU Mensa Architektur (2 euros; payment only by Mensa card)
  • TU Skyline Cafe (3 Euros; payment by cash and Mensa card)
  • TU math building 9th floor canteen (menu, in German) (2.50 Euros)
  • TU math building ground floor canteen (2 Euros)
  • Comprehensive listing

All lunches are self-covered. The prices stated are lower bounds without drinks. This information is still incomplete; it will be completed in the near future.

How to go there?

In order to go there, one can use public transportation. There are several options available:

  • U-Bahn line U2, at the stop U Ernst-Reuter Platz, plus approximately 5 minutes of walking.
  • Buses M45, X9 and 245, at the stop U Ernst-Reuter Platz, plus approximately between 5 and 10 minutes of walking.
  • S-Bahn lines S3, S5 and S7, at the stop S Tiergarten, plus approximately 10 minutes of walking.
  • U-Bahn line U9, at the stop S/U Zoologischer Garten, plus approximately 20 minutes of walking.

One can check precise travelling routes within Berlin public transportation online at fahrinfo.bvg.de.
