10th BMS Student Conference

February 23rd – 25th, 2022

The 10th BMS Student Conference will take place online, directly after the BMS Days, from the 23rd to the 25th of February, 2022. Its main goals are to connect both current and prospective students, help current students learn about each other’s interests, and help prospective students get to know the mathematical landscape in Berlin as well as the social environment at the BMS better. So several BMS students will present part of their mathematical research or thoughts on a variety of topics. Additionally, we will have two talks by the invited speakers, one from Berlin and one external. Every person with an interest in mathematics is welcome to register for free and attend the conference.

Updates

Venue

The conference will take place in room H 3005, in the TU main building and online.
